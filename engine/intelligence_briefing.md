# Liberate.US Scouting Report Summary

## Executive Summary
Liberate.us is a political movement focused on ending the two-party system in the United States. The "American Liberation Movement" has a clearly defined mission and a detailed strategic framework for a national political strike. However, their digital infrastructure is rudimentary, their online presence is nonexistent, and the entire project appears dormant since late 2024.

## What They Have
- **Core Mission**: Dismantle the U.S. two-party political system through nonviolent action. Central document: "New Declaration of American Independence"
- **Strategic Plan**: Step-by-step guide including "Practical Guide to US National Revolution," "Strike Actions," and "Local Coalition-Building"
- **Website Platform**: Built on Wix at liberate.us
- **Call to Action**: "Sign the New Declaration of Independence" - email list acquisition

## What They Need
1. **Project Revitalization**: Website not updated since Oct 18, 2024. Blog and file share empty/non-functional. Need organizer re-engagement.
2. **Digital Presence & Marketing**: Zero social media presence. #LIBERATEUS has minimal traction. Need comprehensive digital marketing strategy across X/Twitter, Facebook, TikTok. Need shareable content.
3. **Technology & Infrastructure**: Wix platform not ideal for growing political movement. Need migration to robust/secure platform with analytics, community forums, fundraising tools.
4. **Community & Trust**: Organizers are anonymous. No community interaction beyond email signup. No social proof. Need transparency, public-facing spokesperson, community tools (forum/Discord), signatories display.

## Conclusion
The core ideas are well-developed and strategic thinking is thorough for a grassroots movement. Currently moribund. Needs professional, sustained effort to resurrect. A strategic partner could re-engage founders, develop modern secure website, launch digital marketing campaign, and implement community-building and fundraising tools.
