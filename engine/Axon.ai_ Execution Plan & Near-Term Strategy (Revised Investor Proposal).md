### 1. Executive Summary: From Wish List to Battle Plan

This document presents a complete redesign of the **Execution Plan & Near-Term Strategy** presentation, directly addressing the panel's verdict of "Dangerously Vague." The previous version was correctly identified as a template, not a plan. This revised structure is a precise, accountable, and executable battle plan.

Every milestone now has a date, an owner, a budget, and measurable success criteria. The plan is designed to pass the panel's "Execution Test": a new employee could read this and know exactly what to do.
