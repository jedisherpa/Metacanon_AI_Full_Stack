# Client Scouting Report: Liberate.US

**Date:** February 15, 2026

## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides a strategic assessment of **Liberate.us**, a political movement focused on ending the two-party system in the United States. The analysis is based on a comprehensive review of their website and digital presence. The movement, which calls itself the "American Liberation Movement," has a clearly defined mission and a detailed strategic framework for a national political strike. However, their digital infrastructure is rudimentary, their online presence is nonexistent, and the entire project appears to have been dormant since late 2024. This presents both a significant challenge and a clear opportunity for a strategic partner to help them revitalize and scale their movement.

## 2. What They Have: A Foundation for Revolution

Liberate.us has established a solid ideological foundation and a basic digital footprint. These assets provide a starting point for any future development.

| Asset Category      | Details                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Core Mission**    | A powerful and clearly articulated mission to dismantle the U.S. two-party political system through nonviolent action. Their central document is the "New Declaration of American Independence," a manifesto that serves as the movement's philosophical core. [1] |
| **Strategic Plan**  | The website provides a surprisingly detailed, step-by-step guide for supporters. This includes a "Practical Guide to US National Revolution," a list of specific "Strike Actions," and a strategy for "Local Coalition-Building." | **Call to Action**  | The primary call to action is to "Sign the New Declaration of Independence," which functions as an email list acquisition tool. This is the main mechanism for capturing supporter interest.                                                              |

## 4. Conclusion & Next Steps

Liberate.us is a concept with a great deal of potential. The core ideas are well-developed, and the strategic thinking is surprisingly thorough for a grassroots movement. However, the project is currently moribund. To succeed, it needs to be resurrected with a professional and sustained effort.

A strategic partner could provide immense value by:

1.  **Re-engaging the founders** to confirm their commitment to the project.
2.  **Developing a new, modern, and secure website** to serve as the movement's digital hub.
3.  **Launching a comprehensive digital marketing and social media campaign** to build an audience and spread the message.
4.  **Implementing community-building and fundraising tools** to foster a sustainable movement.

With the right strategy and execution, Liberate.us could be transformed from a dormant website into a powerful force for political change.
